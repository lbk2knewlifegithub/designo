So, what is the Wall of Fame?

  • Everyone is a mentor. Whether you’re submitting a solution or giving someone feedback, you’re making valuable contributions to the community. Your Mentor Score represents that fact and grows as you contribute.
  • Our Wall of Fame highlights our most active community members each week, month, year, and all-time.

GIVING FEEDBACK

By giving feedback to others, you’re directly helping them improve as a developer. Not only that, but you reinforce your own knowledge on the subject.

Your feedback is marked as helpful
+20 POINTS

AIRDROP RAFFLE

Weekly

At the end of every week, we randomly select a free member on the Wall of Fame to receive a design credit. This can be used to download a design file (Sketch/Figma) for any free challenge.

Monthly

At the end of every month, we randomly select a free member on the Wall of Fame to receive a premium credit. This can be used to download a premium challenge.
